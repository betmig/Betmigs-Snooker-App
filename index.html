How to play?

  • Oi, snooker time! Get that cue ball in the 'D,' cue stays behind. '1' for normal, '2' for reds, '3' for all balls.

  • Now, the plan: reds first, then it's 'follow the rainbow' - Yellow, Green, Brown, Blue, Pink, Black. And don't you dare pot a color too soon, mate, that's -4 points. Plop the cue ball, it's another -4 points. So, stay sharp, 'follow the rainbow,' and let's have a jolly good time on the snooker table!
